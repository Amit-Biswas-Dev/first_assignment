Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before joining DevConf 2026.

Who can attend DevConf 2026?

DevConf 2026 is open to students, software developers, designers, entrepreneurs, and technology enthusiasts of all experience levels.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes. Every registered attendee who participates in the conference will receive a digital certificate after the event.

Are meals and refreshments included?

Lunch, coffee breaks, and light refreshments are included with all conference passes to keep you energized throughout the day.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes. Ticket transfers are allowed until seven days before the event. Simply contact our support team with your registration details.
